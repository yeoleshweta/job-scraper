### Recommended case study to present

**UrbanFit + ABIM combo.** UrbanFit is the *shipped-design-with-metrics* story (counters the "are you really a designer?" probe). ABIM is the *AI/LLM-powered product* story (hits the bonus and shows you can design for the exact problem space Handshake AI sells). Skip pure research stories unless asked.

### Red-flag questions and how to answer

1. **"You've been doing research for the last 4 years — why design now?"**
   *"Research and design have always been the same job for me. At John Deere, I was titled UXR but built the survey platform end-to-end. At UrbanFit and Capita I shipped UI. The reason Handshake's role excites me is that the AI-data space rewards exactly that hybrid — you can't design good labeling workflows without understanding both the operator and the model."*

2. **"Show me your most recent shipped UI."**
   Be ready: ABIM Label Studio config screens + UrbanFit case study. If portfolio doesn't surface these well, fix that *before* applying.

3. **"How do you balance speed vs. craft in an ambiguous environment?"**
   *"I prototype in low-fi first — Figma frames, sometimes paper. Validate the workflow with 3–5 users in a day. Then invest craft only on the screens that survived. UrbanFit's bounce-drop came from killing 4 redesign options early."*

4. **"What's your visa situation?"**
   *"F-1 OPT, 3 years STEM extension runway, no sponsorship needed near-term. Open to relocate to SF."*
