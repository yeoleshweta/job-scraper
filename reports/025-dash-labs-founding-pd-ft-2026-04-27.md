## G) Application Decision

**Decision:** APPLY WITH CAUTION — verify first

**Reason:**
- Genuine fit on end-to-end design + research depth + AI literacy (rare combo).
- Score 3.4/5 reflects three real risks:
  1. **Comp opacity** — no salary band; founding roles can come in at sub-market cash.
  2. **OPT/visa risk** — pre-seed/seed AI startup with 2 co-founders may not be E-Verify enrolled (required for STEM OPT extension). For F-1 OPT base year, employer must be a legitimate registered US employer; "founding team with 4 exits + $100M raised in prior cos" suggests they can run payroll, but legal entity status, US incorporation, and E-Verify must be confirmed before signing.
  3. **Role-archetype drift** — this is a *design-led* founding role, not Shweta's North Star UXR archetype. Apply only if she wants to flex into design-heavy work.

**Pre-application verification checklist:**
- Confirm Dash Labs is US-incorporated and can hire on F-1 OPT (CPT/OPT acceptance).
- Confirm E-Verify enrollment if STEM OPT extension matters in 2027.
- Ask comp band on first call — walk if base is below $100K (profile minimum).
- Ask about research expectations — is the "Founding Product Designer" expected to also run user research, or is that handled? (If yes → fit jumps; if no → archetype mismatch widens.)
