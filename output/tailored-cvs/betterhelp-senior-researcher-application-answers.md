### Why BetterHelp / Why this role

BetterHelp is the world's largest online therapy platform, facilitating millions of sessions between licensed therapists and members in some of the most emotionally sensitive interactions that happen online. Doing user research in that context — where trust, safety, and user wellbeing aren't secondary considerations but the whole point — requires a researcher who takes ethics and rigor seriously. I do. My background in clinical NLP at ABIM (analyzing physician-patient communication behaviors, building bias-detection frameworks for 3,500+ clinical records) has trained me to treat sensitive human interaction data with the care it deserves. Beyond domain fit, BetterHelp's scale — 30,000+ therapists, millions of members — means mixed-methods research here has real impact. Understanding how members find therapists, how they decide to stay, and what makes the experience feel safe enough to be vulnerable is meaningful research that I want to contribute to.
