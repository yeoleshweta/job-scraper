- Designed for deaf/hard-of-hearing individuals, educators, caregivers, and AR/VR developers

### 9. Robust Fake Profile Detection Using Deep Neural Networks with Adversarial Training
**Technologies:** Python, PyTorch, Scikit-Learn, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Foolbox
**Context:** Academic Project | 2025
**GitHub:** [github.com/yeoleshweta/Robust-Fake-Profile-Detection](https://github.com/yeoleshweta/Robust-Fake-Profile-Detection-Using-Deep-Neural-Networks-with-Adversarial-Training)

Deep learning initiative focused on identifying counterfeit Instagram accounts, comparing a baseline DNN trained on legitimate data versus an enhanced variant incorporating adversarial training.

**Technical Details:**
- Dataset: 236 Instagram profiles (28 legitimate, 208 fraudulent) with numerical and boolean features
- Multi-layer feedforward network: 64 input neurons, two hidden layers (32 neurons each) with ReLU activation and dropout regularization (0.2–0.3)
- Adversarial methods: FGSM, PGD, and Carlini-Wagner attacks for robustness testing
- Robust model maintained ~88% accuracy across all adversarial scenarios while standard model degraded significantly
- Balanced precision-recall characteristics preserved under attack conditions
